The Contest

Help name UNDP’s Panda Ambassadors for the Global Goals! You could even win a trip to China to meet them in person…

ENTER NOW!

UNDP has teamed up with the Chengdu Panda Base in China to designate two panda cubs as the first ever ‘Animal Ambassadors for the Global Goals.’

We hope our furry friends will help us raise awareness of the Goals around the world.

But first, they need a name! And that’s where you come in. Have some ideas? Let us know what you think our Panda Ambassadors should be called.

You could even win the trip-of-a-lifetime – a visit to China to meet the pandas in person as a UNDP Panda Champion for the Goals.

  • Contest info

    Choose your favourite Sustainable Development Goal (SDG). If you’re thinking ‘sustainable what?’ - we’ve got you covered - you can learn all about them here.

    So you’ve chosen your favourite Goal. What next? Make us a short video (and we mean short! Any longer than 30 seconds and your entry won’t be considered - click here to see rules in full) that explains what the Goal is, and why it’s important in a fun or creative way. Your video could just be you talking to the camera, or you could get your friends involved (but remember only one pair of names can be submitted per video for the chance to win. Are you an animation whiz? You could even make a cartoon!

    The judges are on the lookout for videos that are fun, creative, and help to explain your chosen Goal to someone who might not know what the SDGs are. Keep this in mind when planning and making your video.

    Oh, we almost forgot - bonus points if your video has a panda theme!

    Once you’ve made your video, upload it to your chosen platform, copy the url and send it to us with your submission. We prefer Youtube but feel free to use any other video hosting provider if Youtube is not available in your country

    Stuck for ideas? UNDP Goodwill Ambassador Michele Yeoh is here to help - she even made her own example entry to give you some inspiration.

    Be sure to check out our frequently asked questions and the full terms and conditions to make sure you have the best chance of catching the judges’ eyes!

    Please note, while uploading a video is optional, only entrants who include a video will be eligible to become one of the 17 panda champions.

  • Requirements

    To have a chance to be selected as one of 17 panda Champions for the Global Goals and win a trip to the Chengdu Panda Base in China, make a video (30 seconds maximum) about your favourite Sustainable Development Goal (don’t know what they are? Read more here) explaining what it is and why it is important in a fun or unusual way. Bonus points if your video has a panda theme!

    Please note, while uploading a video is optional, only entrants who include a video will be eligible to become one of the 17 panda Champions.

    The competition is open until 13 July 2016 at 11:59 p.m. Eastern Standard Time (EST). A panel of judges will select the winning entries for the panda name contest, which will be announced in Chengdu on or before 24 July 2016. The names of the 17 winning Panda Champion entrants will be announced on or before 25 August 2016.
