Top Tips - POP UP BOX?

If you’re unsure what you need to do, here are some helpful hints to get you started

If you’ve thought of your names and are ready to enter, click here.

Want the chance to win a trip to China to meet our Panda Ambassadors and become a Panda Champion for the SDGs? You’ll need to send us a short video!

Don’t panic though - it’s not as hard as it sounds! Follow the steps below

  1. Choose your favourite Sustainable Development Goal (SDG). If you’re thinking ‘sustainable what?’ - we’ve got you covered - you can learn all about them here. LINK TO SDG PAGE
  2. So you’ve chosen your favourite Goal. What next? Make us a short video (and we mean short! Any longer than 30 seconds and your entry won’t be considered - click here to see rules in full) that explains what the Goal is, and why it’s important in a fun or creative way. Your video could just be you talking to the camera, or you could get your friends involved (but remember only one name can be submitted per video for the chance to win - click here to see rules in full). Are you an animation whizz? You could even make a cartoon!
  3. The judges are on the lookout for videos that are fun, creative, and help to explain your chosen Goal to someone who might not know what the SDGs are. Keep this in mind when planning and making your video.
  4. Oh, we almost forgot - bonus points if your video has a panda theme!
  5. Once you’ve made your video, upload it to your chosen platform PLATFORMS? ISSUES?, copy the url and send it to us with your submission. Stuck for ideas? UNDP Goodwill Ambassador Michele Yeoh LINK TO GWA PAGE is here to help - she even made her own example entry LINK TO HER VIDEO to give you some inspiration - you can watch it here. Or check out what others are doing on our featured videos page. LINK TO FEATURED ENTRIES TAB
  6. Be sure to check out our frequently asked questions and the full terms and conditions to make sure you have the best chance of catching the judges’ eye!
  7. Please note, while uploading a video is optional, only entrants who include a video will be eligible to become one of the 17 panda messengers.

OK, I’ve got it and I’m all set to enter LINK TO CONTEST SUBMISSION FORM
