What is a Panda Champion?

Back in January, UNDP and the China’s Chengdu Panda Base announced two baby panda cubs as the first ever Animal Ambassadors for the Sustainable Development Goals.

The cubs were born a few months earlier during the week world leaders convened at the United Nations in New York to adopt the new Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), a bold new agenda that aims at eradicating poverty and tackling some of the most pressing challenges facing the world today.

We’re so excited to be working our adorable Animal Ambassadors to help spread the word.
