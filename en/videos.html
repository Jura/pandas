Videos

In summer 2016, UNDP teamed up with the Chengdu Panda Base in China to launch a global contest to name UNDP’s twin Panda Ambassadors for the Global Goals. A second part of the contest involved making a video about your favorite goal - check out some of the featured entries below.

Contest judges are currently considering all the fantastic ideas we received for names, as well as your wonderful videos. We will soon have some news on an event to announce our winners, so check back here soon!
